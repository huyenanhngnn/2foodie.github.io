PLAY

Food Brings Cultures Together! Vietnam is not just a place you visit; it's a place you experience. And what better way to immerse yourself into the vibrant culture and tradition of this beautiful country than through its food? In this blog, we're excited to take you - curious travellers - on a gastronomic adventure to one of Vietnam's rising culinary gems—4P's, a restaurant that's not just about food, but about bringing people and cultures together.

Pre-dining Experience - Awareness & Consideration

  • The discovery of a hidden gem

    The journey begins long before you show up at Pizza 4P's. The restaurant’s international reputation for serving “the Best Pizza in Asia"[1] with attention from esteemed publications makes it an appealing addition to your bucket list when traveling in Vietnam. Positive online reviews, showcasing images of uniquely crafted pizzas and sharing stories of the restaurant's commitment to sustainability, will further promise you an exceptional local dining experience.

  • Seemless Booking Process

    Once further explored, the user-friendly website of Pizza 4Ps helps you easily learn about its menu and its mission to 'Make the World Smile for Peace'.[2] As the excitement builds, so does the anticipation of tasting the fresh and locally sourced products.

    One tip for a better experience is to always book a table in advance. Pizza 4P’s is notoriously busy during dinner hours, and without a reservation, you might find yourself waiting for a table.[3] Luckily, their online booking system not only provides confirmation via SMS and email but also allows you to communicate specific dietary preferences and special requests, ensuring a personalized dining experience from the start.

  • A heartwarming welcome awaits

    The moment you show up, the promise of a warm welcome is immediately fulfilled. Pizza 4Ps’ staff, proficient in English and trained in the art of “Omotenashi" - the Japanese hospitality spirit[5], will let you know you have a table booked, then they will greet you with smiles and see you seated. As you are escorted to the beautifully set-up tables, the attention to detail in the decor and the cozy setup promise an evening of comfort and culinary delight.

  • Engaging in the art of menu

    The magic lies when the menu arrives. Whether it’s your first visit or your return time, the staff patiently explains the unique offerings of each dish, ensuring that every selection of your order is well-informed and suited to your preferences. Each dish at Pizza 4P's tells a story of creativity and quality consistency[6], blending local ingredients with traditional Italian techniques and a touch of Japanese elements.

During Experience - Decision & Service

  • Celebration of Cultures

    At Pizza 4P’s, the menu is a testament to the harmony between distinctive flavors and cultures - Japanese founders, Italian cuisine, and the Vietnamese market. From traditional Italian pizzas like the Milano Salami & Chorizo Margherita Pizza to fusion dishes like the Bo Kho Pizza or the Teriyaki Chicken Pizza, there is always something to offer to every palate. On the menu, a wide range of food is offered including must-try signature dishes which have created the reputation for 4P’s, and seasonal dishes which are made with fresh produce in season, delivering the taste of nature’s most perfect offerings. Sometimes 4P’s even provides guests with special menus during events such as Earth Day or its 10-year celebration. [6]

  • Unique farm-to-table approach

    Pizza 4P’s is one of the earliest and most successful chains to implement the “farm-to-table” approach to enhance its dining experience. Other than Parma ham and canned tomatoes which cannot be produced locally and have to be sourced directly from Italy, most ingredients such as cheese and greens are made from farms in Dalat[7]. Vegetables are all grown naturally without harmful chemicals and cheeses are transported to restaurants across Vietnam within 24 hours[8] after production, aligning with the restaurant’s commitment to “delivering wow, sharing happiness” and sustainability.

  • Soul of Menu - Signature Dishes

    At 4P’s, customers can order freshly made half & half pizza with the most ordered pizza dishes being the House-made 4 Cheese Pizza and the Salmon Miso Cream Pizza.
    The House-made 4 Cheese Pizza brings to the table the signature color and the pillowy texture of cheese topped with golden-colored drizzled honey. The Salmon Miso Cream Pizza presents slices of salmon mixed with cream cheese garnished with some spring onions. This main course offers a symphony of rich, indulgent flavors with perfectly crisp yet soft crusts, leaving you craving another slice.

    The Crab Tomato Cream Spaghetti with Ricotta Cheese is another dish that makes a name for 4P’s. Its signature presentation of a crab shell on top of the creamy pasta must be familiar to you. The tomato cream sauce is velvety smooth, creating a savory base for the pasta. The sweet, delicate flavor of the crab meat adds delightful oceanic aromas, while the ricotta cheese lends a creamy, slightly tangy finish that ties everything together beautifully.

  • Impressive Multi-Sensory Experience

    A special feature of Pizza 4P’s lies in its various dining space designs with each location being very distinctive yet still maintaining the unique vibe of the chain. The architecture of 4P's Tràng Tiền showcases a harmonious blend of rustic and contemporary design elements.

    The use of natural and raw textures contributes to a relaxed, yet aesthetically pleasing dining experience: High ceilings and large windows make the interior seem more spacious and bathed in natural light. The presence of greenery adds an elegant touch to the atmosphere, along with modern and neutral-colored furniture.

    Interactive design for the sight, hearing, and smell: Wood-fired pizza ovens, with a unique mosaic design, serve as a focal point, emphasizing the made-to-order freshness of the dishes. So customers can enjoy watching their food being prepared while listening to ambient music.

Post Experience - Loyalty & Advocacy

  • When customer voice really matters

    As the final notes of a culinary symphony linger on the palate, the experience at Pizza 4P’s extends beyond the table. After the dining, an iPad is presented as your portal to reflection. This moment of sharing becomes the restaurant’s compass-each critique a beacon guiding their journey to perfection.

    “We'd do anything we can to provide kaizen to them. Whenever we receive feedback, even if it's just from one single person, we will try to make sure we can reach out to that guest. We will do anything we can to keep them as guests.”[10]

  • "Earth to People" Principle

    For the conscious customer, Pizza 4P's is more than a destination; it's a declaration—a pledge that good taste and good ethics need not be mutually exclusive. The promise of Pizza 4P's is that every visit is an act of participation in a greener, kinder world. And in this promise, they find a compelling reason to return, time and time again, loyalty deepening with the understanding that here, deliciousness and sustainability dine hand in hand.

  • Dialogue of sustainability

    In Pizza 4P’s, the commitment to sustainability is not a silent practice but a dialogue, a promise spoken with each bite of locally sourced fare.

    Pizza 4P’s compels not with clamor, but with a quiet assurance that here, within these walls, your presence is part of a larger purpose. It is knowing that with each return, you are part of a cycle of sustainability, a circle of community, a symphony of flavors orchestrated not just for the moment, but for the tomorrow.

  • Ending note

    As a traveler in Vietnam, to dine at Pizza 4P’s is to know that you will leave only to return, drawn back by the magnetic promise of a meal that satisfies much more than appetite—it satisfies the soul’s yearning for good, done well, and shared with the world.
